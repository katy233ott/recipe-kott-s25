Swole Balls of Glory

The BEST Preworkout Power Poppers

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop protien powder (any flavor-excpet sadness)
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butt (or almond butter if you're bougie)
  • 1 banana (overripe, like your gym socks)
  • 1/2 cup oats (because you're civilized)
  • A dash of cinnamon (for flair and fake fitness influencer vibes)

Instructions

  1. Throw the banana into a bowl. Smash it like your deadlift PR. Pretend it's someone who told you "cardio is better than lifting"...gross.
  2. Add the peanut butter and mix until it's smooth enough to make you question why this isn't dessert.
  3. Toss in the oats and protien powder. Stir until it looks like something your caveman ancestors would hoard.
  4. Sprinkle cinnamon like you're a wizard casting spells of gains. Optional: whisper "swole" after every shake.
  5. Roll into bite-sized, refrigerate for 15 mins (or eat immediately if you lack self-control and self-respect). Consume before your workout and feel your muscles whisper, "thank you".
